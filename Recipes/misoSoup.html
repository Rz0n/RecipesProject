Miso Soup Recipe

Description

This is miso soup is full of savory flavors.You can use yallow, white or red miso for this soup.Yallow is move sweet and creamy, red miso is stronger and saltier.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of water
  • 2 teaspoons dashi granules
  • 3 tablespoons miso paste
  • 1 (8 ounce) package silken tofu, diced
  • 2 green onions, slice diagonally into 1/2 inch pieces

Steps

  1. Combine water dashi granules in medium soucepan over medium-high heat, bring to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat to medium and whisk in miso paste.
  3. Stir in tofu.
  4. Separate the layers of green onions, and add them do the soup.
  5. Simmer gently for 2 to 3 minutes before serving.
